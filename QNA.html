    A: At Bunny Toons Studios, we find it a red flag to include LGBTQ+ themes in Stormy and Hops: The Adventure of a Duel Rabbit or any of our future projects, as we believe it could have an impact on both adults and children. We may never include such content, as our focus is on fun, friendship, and adventure. As stated on our official Twitter/X page, Stormy and Hops does not include LGBTQ+ themes and never will. Our priority is to create content that remains enjoyable and comfortable for a wide audience.
