Stormy And Hops: Characters

characters

Main Characters

Species: Rabbit | Gender: Male | Lives: The forest of Toon Springs in an RV | First Appearance: A New Toon Beginning S1 E1

Biography: A rabbit with red fur, about 14 years old. He has a white tail and loves wearing his orange shirt with the letter S on it. He enjoys drinking Carrot Spring Soda day or night.

Personality: A kind and soft type of rabbit. He loves everything he does and tries to lift people up, making them feel good. He is a very kind bunny.

Likes: Adventures, Being with Hops, Toon Springs, Carrot Spring Soda

Dislikes: Being an orphan, Scarlet being annoying to Hops

Friends: Hops Bunny (Best Friend), Oscar the Crazy Scientist, Paul the Hyper Cat, Scout Bunny, Katie the Deer, Mr. Lucky, Cloudy Kitty, Scout Bunny

Enemies: Scarlet Bunny, Preston (sometimes)

Relationships of Friends/Enemies:

  • Hops Bunny: Hops and Stormy are a dynamic duo who explore and go on adventures together. They met when Stormy was seven years old, walking around the forest near Toon Springs. Their friendship has grown very strong. Hops has a crush on Stormy, but it’s unclear if he feels the same way.
  • Oscar (The Crazy Scientist): Stormy met Oscar before Hops, when he was seven. Oscar has helped Stormy with many things, including the red rocket near his house.
  • Scarlet Bunny: Scarlet also has a crush on Stormy. Stormy doesn’t feel the same way about her, but he notices the tension between Hops and Scarlet every day. He chooses to focus on having fun with Hops instead and doesn’t like Scarlet at all.
  • Mr. Lucky (Flamingo): Mr. Lucky helps Stormy sometimes, though their connection isn’t very strong.
  • Paul the Hyper Cat: Stormy and Paul are pretty good friends. Stormy actually talked to Paul when they needed help in a game, especially when Scarlet caused trouble and Hops needed support.
  • Mrs. Diamond: Stormy and Mrs. Diamond are good friends. He really likes her cooking.
  • Preston (The Big Dumb Rock): Stormy doesn’t see Preston as a threat. He thinks of him as a little rock that wants to give hugs, even though Preston sometimes calls him a stupid rabbit. Stormy tries to hug him anyway.

  • Cloudy Kitty: TBA

Species: Rabbit | Gender: Female | Lives: Toon Springs | First Appearance: A New Toon Beginning

Biography: A Bunny with her baby blue skirt and pink shirt with yellow fur on her that likes to go out with Stormy on adventures. She is nice, but when it comes to the game, sometimes she gets a little bit interested in winning until Storm tells her that’s not what it’s all about. She likes doing a lot of stuff, maybe sometimes dancing, maybe even with Stormy sometimes.

Personality: She is generally nice and friendly. She can be a little competitive during games, but usually listens when Stormy reminds her that it’s about having fun. She is curious, active, and enjoys participating in different activities.

Likes: Hanging out with Stormy, Dancing, Adventures, Being nice, Football

Dislikes: Scarlet

Friends: Stormy Bunny (crush), Oscar the Crazy Scientist, Paul the Hyper Cat, Scout Bunny, Katie the Deer, Mr. Lucky, Cloudy Kitty, Scout

Enemies: Scarlet Bunny (rivals), Preston (sometimes)

Relationships of Friends/Enemies:

  • Stormy Bunny: Stormy and Hops are always going on adventures together. Stormy is the one that Hops has the crush on the most, which is something he notices.
  • Scarlet Bunny: A huge rival/enemy to Hops because Hops and Scarlet like Stormy a lot. Scarlet really doesn’t like Hops, and Hops doesn’t like Scarlet either.
  • Oscar (The Crazy Scientist): Hops likes it whenever he creates inventions for Stormy and her crazy wild adventures. They’re good friends with Stormy as well.
  • Paul the Hyper Cat: Hops and Paul are pretty good friends. They needed Paul’s help during Yellow versus Purple games.
  • Katie the Deer: Hops met Katie in Yellow versus Purple to help cheer on their team and support their football games.
  • Preston (The Big Dumb Rock): Just like with Stormy, Hops sees Preston as harmless. She tries to give him hugs even though he calls her a stupid rabbit.
  • Cloudy Kitty: TBA
  • Scout Bunny: TBA
  • Mrs. Diamond: Hops loves Mrs. Diamond‘s cooking. She always heads down to Mrs. Diamond’s bakery to get something delicious to eat because it’s so good. “She’s the best cook in Toon Springs,” she says.

Species: Rabbit | Gender: Female | First Appearance: Beginning

Scarlet Bunny is a 14-year-old purple rabbit who wears a violet shirt and blue skirt. She has a crush on Stormy and constantly tries to win his attention, often causing trouble for Hops in the process.

Personality: Scarlet can be mean and manipulative, except when it comes to Stormy. She's determined to get what she wants and doesn't like anyone who helps Hops. She's a bit crazy and persistent in her schemes.

Friends: Stormy (sometimes), The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy

Enemies: Hops Bunny (main rival), Paul the Hyper Cat, Mr. Lucky (Flamingo), The Crazy Scientist, Mrs. Diamond

Romance: Has a crush on Stormy and competes with Hops for his attention. May have feelings developing with The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy.

Species: Rock | Gender: Male | First Appearance: Beginning

Preston thinks of himself as an evil genius, but he's not quite as smart as he believes. Though he considers himself a threat, Hops and Stormy don't see him as particularly dangerous. He secretly has a crush on Scarlet Bunny.

Personality: Preston is not very bright despite thinking he's a genius. He's more of a comic relief character who provides amusing situations rather than real danger.

Friends: Scarlet Bunny (crush)

Enemies: Stormy and Hops (though not very effectively), Paul the Hyper Cat (when annoyed)

Romance: Has a secret crush on Scarlet Bunny and may form an "evil pair" with her, though his stupidity often gets in the way.

Species: Cat | Gender: Male | First Appearance: Episode 1: A New Toon Beginning

Paul the Hyper Cat is a 13-year-old orange cat known for his boundless energy and wild imagination. Originally a YouTube sensation, he joined the cartoon and constantly acts like he's in a livestream, talking to imaginary fans.

Personality: Paul is hyperactive, enthusiastic, dramatic, and theatrical. He talks fast, moves fast, and thinks even faster. He's convinced he's internet famous and loves pulling off "epic" stunts. Despite his chaos, he's kind-hearted and surprisingly helpful when needed.

Friends: Stormy Bunny, Hops Bunny, The Crazy Scientist, Mr. Lucky, Mrs. Diamond

Enemies: Scarlet Bunny (occasionally), Preston (when annoyed)

Special Note: Based on the real Paul the Hyper Cat YouTube channel and voiced by Cat Man (the channel owner)!

Species: Deer | Gender: Female

Katie the Deer is a white deer with antlers who's known for being a "smart cookie." She's part of the cheerleading department at Toons Springs University.

Personality: Katie is intelligent, kind, and academically oriented. She brings a different perspective to the group with her university background and cheerleading experience.

Special Role: Represents the academic side of their world and shows that adventure characters can also excel in school activities!

Supporting Characters

Species: Human | Gender: Male

The Crazy Scientist is a human who wears a white lab coat and is a loyal friend to both Stormy and Hops. He's the genius behind Stormy's red rocket that they use for daily adventures.

Personality: Inventive, helpful, and always ready to create gadgets to help his rabbit friends on their adventures.

Role: The tech support of the group, providing vehicles and inventions for their adventures.

Species: Flamingo | Gender: Male

Mr. Lucky is a teacher with pink feathers and glasses who works at the school attended by our main characters.

Personality: Professional educator who helps guide the characters in their learning journey.

Role: Represents the educational aspect of the characters' lives outside of their adventures.

Species: Dog | Gender: Female

Mrs. Diamond is a brown dog with black spots who's known for her incredible culinary skills. She runs a bakery that the characters visit.

Personality: Warm, welcoming, and passionate about cooking. Hops especially admires her amazing food.

Role: The community's baker who provides delicious treats and a cozy gathering place for the characters.
