Characters

Meet the characters of Stormy and Hops!

Main Characters

Stormy Bunny

Species: Rabbit

Gender: Male

First Appearance: Beginning

Voiced By: (Unknown)

Friends:

  • Hops
  • The Crazy Scientist (friend)
  • Mr. Lucky (the Flamingo)
  • Mrs. Diamond
  • Paul the hyper cat
  • Hops' mom

Enemies:

  • Scarlet Bunny
  • The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy (sometimes at a certain extent)

Biography: Stormy is a 14-year-old red-furred rabbit who is friendly and attractive. He grew up with an unknown past and woke up one day near a bird’s nest, which became his temporary home. With Hops by his side, he embarks on countless adventures. Although his history remains unclear, his adventures with Hops are full of excitement and mystery. He is kind-hearted and a bit of an adventurer.

Personality: Stormy is brave, caring, and always ready to help his friends. While he doesn’t openly talk about his past, his heartwarming attitude makes him beloved by all. He's also a bit naive but learns and grows with each challenge he faces.

Relationships: Friends:

  • Hops Bunny: Stormy and Hops are a dynamic duo that explores and embarks on adventures together. They met when Stormy was young, and their friendship has grown strong. Hops has a crush on Stormy, but it's inconclusive if he feels the same way. She finds him incredibly pretty with his yellow fur and pink shirt, along with pink hair on top.
  • The Crazy Scientist: A human who has been a friend to Stormy, helping him by making a red rocket that he uses daily.
  • Scarlet Bunny: Stormy does not pay attention to Scarlet's crush on him, though Scarlet tries to compete with Hops for his affection.
  • Mr. Lucky (Flamingo): A teacher with pink feathers and glasses. (no other details)
  • Paul the Silent Cat: He enjoys hanging out with Paul at school, playing and having fun. Paul only likes to hang out with Stormy and Hops, as he's generally silent and distant from others.
  • Mrs. Diamond: A dog known for her culinary skills. Stormy, along with Hops, admires her amazing cooking.
Enemies:
  • The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy: Although he thinks of himself as a genius, Stormy and Hops don’t see him as a real threat. He believes he is far more intelligent than he is, often creating chaos without actually succeeding in his plans.

Family:

  • Hops Bunny Mom: A kind and caring rabbit with yellow fur who raised Hops. She is a positive influence on Stormy as well.

Romance: A developing connection between Stormy and Hops, with each episode bringing them closer. But the romantic dynamics remain a bit unclear as they grow as friends.

Quotes:

  • "Let's take on this adventure together, Hops!"
  • "I never knew how much fun we could have until I met you, Hops."

Hops Bunny

Species: Rabbit

Gender: Female

First Appearance: Beginning

Voiced By: (Unknown)

Friends:

  • Stormy
  • The Crazy Scientist
  • Mr. Lucky (the Flamingo)
  • Mrs. Diamond
  • Paul the silent cat

Enemies:

  • Scarlet Bunny
  • The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy (sometimes)

Biography: Hops is a 14-year-old, yellow rabbit with pink hair, who is sweet, caring, and intelligent. She has a crush on Stormy, though he is unaware of her feelings. Hops enjoys adventures with Stormy and has a rivalry with Scarlet Bunny, who also likes Stormy.

Personality: Hops is known for her kindness and intelligence. She’s always supportive of Stormy, and though she can be frustrated by Scarlet Bunny's attempts to win Stormy’s heart, she remains strong.

Relationships: Friends:

  • Stormy Bunny: Hops and Stormy are inseparable companions. While Stormy is oblivious to her feelings, Hops quietly supports him through every adventure they face.
  • The Crazy Scientist: The Crazy Scientist is one of Hops’ allies, although she doesn’t quite understand all the scientist's odd inventions.
  • Mr. Lucky (Flamingo): A school teacher, admired by Hops for his wisdom, despite his peculiarities.
  • Mrs. Diamond: A wise older figure who offers guidance, especially on culinary topics. Hops looks up to her.
  • Paul the Silent Cat: A quiet companion who often just observes rather than actively engaging in adventures.
Enemies:
  • Scarlet Bunny: Hops and Scarlet Bunny are rivals in love, with Hops feeling frustrated by Scarlet’s attempts to get Stormy’s attention.
  • The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy: Though not always an immediate threat, Hops still has to outsmart him at times.

Romance: Hops’ crush on Stormy drives much of her actions, though she tries to hide her feelings. There is a deep bond between them, but the direction of their relationship remains in the air.

Quotes:

  • "Adventures are always better when we're together, Stormy!"
  • "You can't force someone's heart, Scarlet. Love should come naturally."

Scarlet Bunny

Species: Rabbit

Gender: Female

First Appearance: Beginning

Voiced By: (Unknown)

Friends:

  • Stormy (sometimes)
  • The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy

Enemies:

  • Hops Bunny
  • Paul the Silent Cat
  • Mr. Lucky (the Flamingo)
  • The Crazy Scientist
  • Mrs. Diamond

Biography: Scarlet is a 14-year-old purple rabbit who wears a violet shirt, a blue skirt, and has a red mouth. She has a crush on Stormy Bunny and constantly tries to win his attention, even though he is unaware of her feelings. Her rival is Hops Bunny, who also likes Stormy. Scarlet isn’t very nice, and her obsession often leads her into trouble.

Personality: Scarlet is mean, especially toward Hops and the others, but oddly kind to Stormy. She wants to be part of adventures just to get closer to him, though she’s a bit wild and unstable. She doesn’t like the Crazy Scientist because he supports Hops.

Relationships: Stormy Bunny: Scarlet is always trying to separate Stormy from Hops. Though she appears in almost every episode, Stormy and Hops usually try to escape her.

Hops Bunny: The rivalry is fierce. Scarlet constantly tries to steal Stormy’s attention, leading to frequent conflicts with Hops. She often mocks Hops and has even said, “I hate that rabbit so much!”

The Crazy Scientist: A human ally of Hops and Stormy. Scarlet dislikes him for helping Hops and has no interest in his inventions.

Mr. Lucky (Flamingo): Scarlet doesn't like school or Mr. Lucky. She finds him annoying.

Paul the Silent Cat: Scarlet doesn’t get along with Paul. He never talks to her, likely because of how she treats Hops.

Mrs. Diamond: Scarlet rarely visits her bakery and finds her annoying.

The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy: Scarlet feels like he might have a crush on her. She enjoys his evil nature, but finds him too dumb to be useful.

Family: Unknown

Romance: Scarlet and the Evil Rock may be an evil pair, but Scarlet doesn’t seem sure about her feelings. She might like him—or maybe not.

Preston (The Big Dumb Rock)

Species: Rock (literally just a rock that can talk and is dumb)

Gender: Male

First Appearance: Beginning

Voiced By: (Unknown)

Friends:

  • Stormy (sometimes)
  • Hops Bunny (sometimes)
  • Scarlet Bunny

Enemies:

  • Stormy Bunny (sometimes)
  • Hops Bunny (sometimes)
  • Paul the Silent Cat
  • Mr. Lucky (the Flamingo)
  • The Crazy Scientist
  • Mrs. Diamond

Biography: A rock that thinks he’s evil, but is really just a harmless, goofy lump of stone.

Personality: Preston believes he’s a genius evil villain, but in reality, he’s clueless. He says nonsense like "1 + 1 = triangle" and thinks the Earth is flat. He’s delusional, dramatic, and hugs people while pretending to be sinister.

Relationships: Stormy Bunny: Stormy tries to hug Preston all the time, which drives him crazy. He often calls Stormy “stupid rabbit.”

Hops Bunny: Hops doesn’t treat him like a villain, which frustrates him. He complains that she calls him “just a dumb rock” when he wants to be seen as evil.

The Crazy Scientist: Preston doesn’t care much about him, but knows he helps Stormy and Hops.

Mr. Lucky (Flamingo): Mr. Lucky always stops Preston’s chaos at school, which makes Preston feel like a failure.

Paul the Silent Cat: Paul never talks to Preston and seems to ignore him entirely. Preston yells about being an “evil villain” but Paul doesn’t care.

Mrs. Diamond: Preston thinks her food is okay. He sometimes visits her bakery and grabs a snack.

Scarlet Bunny: Preston really likes Scarlet. He says she’s amazing and doesn’t think she’s mean. He has a big crush on her.

Family: Unknown

Romance: Preston thinks he and Scarlet make a great evil pair, but he’s unsure if she actually likes him back. Still, he has strong feelings for her.
