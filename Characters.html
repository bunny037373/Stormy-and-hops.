Stormy And Hops: Characters

Meet the characters of our adventure!

Main Characters

Species: Rabbit | Gender: Male | First Appearance: Beginning

Stormy Bunny is a red rabbit who wears an orange shirt with an "S" on it. He's 14 years old and embarks on exciting adventures with his best friend Hops. Stormy uses a red rocket (made by The Crazy Scientist) for daily adventures and exploration.

Personality: Stormy is brave, adventurous, and always ready to explore new places. He's kind-hearted and loyal to his friends, though he's unaware that both Hops and Scarlet have crushes on him.

Friends: Hops Bunny (best friend), The Crazy Scientist, Mr. Lucky (Flamingo), Mrs. Diamond, Paul the Hyper Cat, Hops' Mom

Enemies: Scarlet Bunny (sometimes), The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy (occasionally)

Romance: A developing romantic connection with Hops Bunny grows stronger with each episode!

Species: Rabbit | Gender: Female | First Appearance: Beginning

Hops Bunny is a 14-year-old yellow rabbit with pink hair who wears a pink shirt and baby blue skirt. She's Stormy's adventure companion and has a secret crush on him. She finds him incredibly pretty and loves going on adventures together.

Personality: Hops is sweet, caring, kind, and intelligent. She gets along with most characters and is known for her determination and strength, even when facing challenges. She's the voice of reason in their adventures.

Friends: Stormy Bunny (crush), The Crazy Scientist, Mr. Lucky (Flamingo), Mrs. Diamond, Paul the Hyper Cat, her Mom

Enemies: Scarlet Bunny (nemesis), The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy (sometimes)

Famous Quote: "Adventures are always better when we're together, Stormy!"

Romance: Has a crush on Stormy, and their romantic connection develops throughout the series!

Species: Rabbit | Gender: Female | First Appearance: Beginning

Scarlet Bunny is a 14-year-old purple rabbit who wears a violet shirt and blue skirt. She has a crush on Stormy and constantly tries to win his attention, often causing trouble for Hops in the process.

Personality: Scarlet can be mean and manipulative, except when it comes to Stormy. She's determined to get what she wants and doesn't like anyone who helps Hops. She's a bit crazy and persistent in her schemes.

Friends: Stormy (sometimes), The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy

Enemies: Hops Bunny (main rival), Paul the Hyper Cat, Mr. Lucky (Flamingo), The Crazy Scientist, Mrs. Diamond

Romance: Has a crush on Stormy and competes with Hops for his attention. May have feelings developing with The Stupid Evil Rock Bad Guy.

Species: Rock | Gender: Male | First Appearance: Beginning

Preston thinks of himself as an evil genius, but he's not quite as smart as he believes. Though he considers himself a threat, Hops and Stormy don't see him as particularly dangerous. He secretly has a crush on Scarlet Bunny.

Personality: Preston is not very bright despite thinking he's a genius. He's more of a comic relief character who provides amusing situations rather than real danger.

Friends: Scarlet Bunny (crush)

Enemies: Stormy and Hops (though not very effectively), Paul the Hyper Cat (when annoyed)

Romance: Has a secret crush on Scarlet Bunny and may form an "evil pair" with her, though his stupidity often gets in the way.

Species: Cat | Gender: Male | First Appearance: Episode 1: A New Toon Beginning

Paul the Hyper Cat is a 13-year-old orange cat known for his boundless energy and wild imagination. Originally a YouTube sensation, he joined the cartoon and constantly acts like he's in a livestream, talking to imaginary fans.

Personality: Paul is hyperactive, enthusiastic, dramatic, and theatrical. He talks fast, moves fast, and thinks even faster. He's convinced he's internet famous and loves pulling off "epic" stunts. Despite his chaos, he's kind-hearted and surprisingly helpful when needed.

Friends: Stormy Bunny, Hops Bunny, The Crazy Scientist, Mr. Lucky, Mrs. Diamond

Enemies: Scarlet Bunny (occasionally), Preston (when annoyed)

Special Note: Based on the real Paul the Hyper Cat YouTube channel and voiced by Cat Man (the channel owner)!

Species: Deer | Gender: Female

Katie the Deer is a white deer with antlers who's known for being a "smart cookie." She's part of the cheerleading department at Toons Springs University.

Personality: Katie is intelligent, kind, and academically oriented. She brings a different perspective to the group with her university background and cheerleading experience.

Special Role: Represents the academic side of their world and shows that adventure characters can also excel in school activities!

Supporting Characters

Species: Human | Gender: Male

The Crazy Scientist is a human who wears a white lab coat and is a loyal friend to both Stormy and Hops. He's the genius behind Stormy's red rocket that they use for daily adventures.

Personality: Inventive, helpful, and always ready to create gadgets to help his rabbit friends on their adventures.

Role: The tech support of the group, providing vehicles and inventions for their adventures.

Species: Flamingo | Gender: Male

Mr. Lucky is a teacher with pink feathers and glasses who works at the school attended by our main characters.

Personality: Professional educator who helps guide the characters in their learning journey.

Role: Represents the educational aspect of the characters' lives outside of their adventures.

Species: Dog | Gender: Female

Mrs. Diamond is a brown dog with black spots who's known for her incredible culinary skills. She runs a bakery that the characters visit.

Personality: Warm, welcoming, and passionate about cooking. Hops especially admires her amazing food.

Role: The community's baker who provides delicious treats and a cozy gathering place for the characters.

Species: Rabbit | Gender: Female

Hops' Mom is a kind and caring rabbit with yellow fur, just like her daughter. She's supportive of Hops' friendship with Stormy and their adventures.

Personality: Loving, nurturing, and understanding. She provides a stable home base for the adventurous characters.

Role: The parental figure who offers wisdom and support when the young adventurers need guidance.
